I am a self-taught comic writer and artist. I draw with pencil, pen/brush, and ink, usually on Bristol. I was inspired to make sequential art after discovering Jhonen Vasquez's Johnny the Homicidal Maniac in 1996. In addition to Mr. Vasquez, my comic heroes are Ted Naifeh, Aaron Alexovich, and Mike Mignola.

Art is how I get to see the shadow world.
